1 Social Media Posting

Show up where divers scroll

Most divers plan trips on their phones — bouncing between Instagram, Facebook and TikTok. A steady, beautiful feed keeps Casa Escondida top-of-mind and builds the trust that turns a follow into a booking.

  • Stay top-of-mind. Consistent posting keeps you in the feed between trips, so you're the name they remember when they book.
  • Turn followers into guests. Every post is a soft invitation — reels, tips and guest moments that drive DMs and enquiries.
  • Free reach that compounds. Guest tags, shares and saves widen your audience without spending a peso on ads.

2 Website

A home that turns lookers into bookers

Social gets divers curious — the website is where they decide. A fast, beautiful, easy-to-book site (that AI assistants can read and recommend) is the difference between a lost tab and a confirmed reservation.

  • Fast & mobile-first. Divers browse on phones with patchy signal — a lightweight site loads instantly and keeps them reading.
  • Direct bookings. A clear enquiry & booking path means more reservations that skip the OTA commission.
  • Found by Google & AI. Structured, keyword-rich pages rank in search and get named by ChatGPT, Gemini & AI Overviews.
